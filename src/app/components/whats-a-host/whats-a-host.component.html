What's a host?

Hosts are people who invite others into their lives and into a closer relationship with God. This could be you doing a bible study on your back porch, or watching a service with others in your living room, or maybe just meeting your group for coffee. As a host, you will pick the place to meet, coordinate the times, and then be present. You don't have to be a spiritual guru, just interested in following God yourself and helping others do the same. If that's you then let's do this thing.
